About Me

I've been exactly where you are now

My Story

It started with my own sleepless nights

I remember the long days and the even longer nights. Pushing my baby in the pram in the middle of the night, and taking turns with my husband holding her in the rocking chair because it was the only way she would sleep. I tried everything, yet nothing seemed to work. But I was desperate for something to, so I kept going.

I researched, I stayed persistent, and eventually my daughter slept. When she did, our whole family changed with it.

Why It Matters

What good sleep gave us back

I'm a full-time mum of 3 children under 6, and for the first few years, 2 under 2. I know exactly what busy, beautiful family life truly looks like, and I get to experience the good chaos of it all because we get the sleep we all need.

What I've learned is that children genuinely thrive on routine, and so do I. That rhythm wasn't luck. It was intentional, and it changed everything for us. It's exactly the sense of ease I want to help create for your family.

My Background

Qualified, experienced, and a mum who truly gets it

Alongside being a mum of three, I have worked in the childcare sector for over ten years. I hold a Bachelor of Social Welfare and a Diploma of Community Services and I am deeply passionate about helping families find calm, confidence and rest. Everything I do is personal, practical and made entirely for you.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this safe for my baby?
Yes. Everything I suggest is grounded in safe sleeping guidelines and gentle, age-appropriate practice. I am not a medical professional, so if I ever feel something should be checked by your GP or paediatrician, I will always say so.
What if it doesn't work?
Every child is different, and I can't promise an exact result or timeframe, no honest supporter can. What I can promise is my full support, realistic guidance, and that I'll be right beside you. Consistency makes a big difference, and we'll troubleshoot together along the way.
How do we start?
Choose the service that feels right, complete a short intake form, and I'll take it from there, reviewing everything and guiding you step by step. If you're not sure which option suits you, just send me a message and we'll work it out together.
My baby is a newborn, can you help?
For little ones under 4 months, a structured routine isn't developmentally expected yet, so instead I offer gentle, personal advice and reassurance to support those early weeks. Reach out and I'll point you in the right direction.
Where are you based, and do you travel?
I'm in the Illawarra, NSW. My Hands-On at Home visits are for local families, and everything else (the Sleep Check and Complete Sleep Reset) is offered online, Australia-wide.
How and when do I pay?
Payment is made in full upfront to confirm your booking, via bank transfer. You will receive an invoice and a confirmation email with your next steps.
What's your cancellation policy?
A full refund is available only if you cancel before I have begun reviewing your intake form or started any work on your plan. Once I have started, fees are non-refundable as your payment covers my time and expertise. Rather than cancelling, you are welcome to reschedule once so your payment is not lost. If your child is unwell, please let me know and we will rearrange with no penalty.
